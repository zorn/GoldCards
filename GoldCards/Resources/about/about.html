Created by: Clickable Bliss

Follow the app on twitter: @GoldCardsApp

GoldCards is a Hearthstone reference guide. Using GoldCards you can:

  • Browse and search all heroes, cards, quest, achievements and game mechanics of Hearthstone.
  • View card info including the rare animated gold card versions.
